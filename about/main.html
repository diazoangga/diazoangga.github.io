About Me

Hello! I'm Diaz Angga Permana, a passionate AI researcher and software developer. My interests lie in machine learning, computer vision, and mathematical modeling.

I love exploring how mathematical theory supports modern AI and how we can leverage that to build robust applications.

Outside of tech, I enjoy sharing knowledge, writing articles, and continuously learning.

My journey into AI began in 2016, when I wrote about an intelligent control system for traffic lights and had it published in 2017. Then, in 2019 I started my career as an automation engineer at Subaru after earning my Bachelor's degree. Over three years in the industry performing automation tasks, I found myself increasingly drawn to the world of AI in automation. After dedicating time to online courses and self-study, I made the leap into AI engineering.

In 2021, I started a Medium blog to document my project experiments and break down complex research papers into digestible insights. It became a way to share knowledge and refine my understanding.
